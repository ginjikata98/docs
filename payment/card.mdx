## Step-by-Step Payment Process

### Step 1: Go to Payment Portal

Visit: **[dopaminegirl.com/app/top-up](https://dopaminegirl.com/app/top-up)**

### Step 2: Choose Your Payment Method

- **Recommended**: Select Stripe (for Visa cards) or PayPal
- **Alternative**: Choose any other method from the list above

### Step 3: Enter Payment Information

**For Stripe (Visa Cards):**

- Card number (16 digits)
- Expiry date (MM/YY)
- CVV code (3 digits on back)
- Your billing address

**For PayPal:**

- Click PayPal button
- Log into your PayPal account
- Confirm the payment

**For Bank Transfer:**

- Select your bank
- Follow your bank's login process
- Confirm the payment amount

### Step 4: Complete & Confirm

- Review all details carefully
- Click confirm to process payment
- Save your confirmation receipt

### Step 5: Get Your Credits

- **Instant methods**: Credits appear immediately
- **Delayed methods**: Credits appear after waiting period (see table above)

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### ❌ Payment Declined?

1. **Check card details** - Make sure everything is typed correctly
2. **Check your balance** - Ensure you have enough money
3. **Contact your bank** - They might be blocking the payment
4. **Try a different method** - Use PayPal or another option

### ⏳ Payment Taking Too Long?

1. **Check processing time** - Some methods have waiting periods
2. **Look for confirmation email** - Check your inbox/spam
3. **Contact support** - If it's been longer than expected

### 🌍 Method Not Available?

1. **Try Stripe or PayPal** - These work in most countries
2. **Check your region** - Some methods are country-specific
3. **Use alternatives** - Consider crypto or Telegram payments
